About our Station

Pirate Radio 104.7 is a non-profit radio station based out of Greeley, CO. The morning show runs from 6am-9am every weekday that features a question of the day where listeners can call and answer! Host and owner, George Gray, has been runnning the station for over 10 years! The main goal for this station to shine a light on local ma and pa businesses that can flourish with help from the community! Continue reading to learn more!
